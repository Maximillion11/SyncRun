Restart - Restarts from scratch
Play - Lets you watch any recorded movement
Stop - Stops and saves your active player AND stops any playing recordings

Red square at top = player not recorded
Green square at top = player with recording
White square at top = active player

Clicking on the squares at the top will remove any recording on that player, 
give you control of that player and play any existing recorded players 
so you can interact with them.

A + D to move
SPACE to jump and double jump

Recorded data is saved in %UserProfile%\AppData\LocalLow\DefaultCompany\SyncRun\SavedData
